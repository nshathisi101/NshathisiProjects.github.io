Masa Mobile Application

The intelligent mobile application categorizes threats into three classes: normal, medium, and high. If the mobile application identifies hate keywords at a low pitch say 1000hz it allows parties involved a chance to resolve issues by themselves, higher pitch triggers various levels of assistance starting from the 5 trusted emergency contacts, ending with police interference respectively. One of the supporting features of the intelligent mobile application is that it provides a platform where victims of Gender Based Violence can discuss issues among themselves anonymously and with a choice of involving social workers. Mpatisa Ntlo

Real estate shopping is rapidly becoming a significant shopping activity. Landlords advertise their properties on social media platforms while prospective tenants search for property on social media. Prospective tenants spend hours collecting and reviewing various real estate advertising publications. The information that is on social media is diverse and unstructured, separating social media information according to a particular domain is a challenging task. Conducting a search process to find relevant information on social media can be a complex task. Most existing tools used to search for information are widely inadequate since they do not personalize data. Most users do not know how to map their preferences to a query that finds a match that closely meet their requirements.
